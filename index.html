Everything you need
Built for real life,
not just the office.
DayFlow brings together everything you need to stay on top of your day — whether you're at work, at home, or somewhere in between.
📋
Visual Kanban Board
Drag and drop tasks between To Do, In Progress and Done. See your entire day at a glance with colour-coded priority indicators.
🌐
Network & Collaboration
Invite friends, family or colleagues to your network. Assign tasks to each other and stay in sync without messy group chats.
📎
File Attachments
Attach images, PDFs, documents and more directly to tasks. Everything related to a task lives in one place.
📊
Stats & Insights
Track how long tasks take to complete, view breakdowns by category and priority, and measure your productivity over time.
📤
Share Tasks Instantly
Share any task via WhatsApp, email or a copied link. Perfect for keeping others in the loop without switching apps.
🔔
Smart Notifications
Get notified when someone assigns you a task, accepts your invite, or completes work you assigned to them.
